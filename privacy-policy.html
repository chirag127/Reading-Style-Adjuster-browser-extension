Introduction

Reading Style Adjuster is a browser extension that empowers users to personalize and standardize the appearance of text content across websites. This privacy policy explains how the extension handles user data.

Required Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

storage

This permission is required to save your settings locally on your device. We use the browser's chrome.storage.local API to store:

  • Your style preferences (font size, font family, line height, word spacing, text alignment)
  • Saved profiles
  • Site exceptions and site-specific profiles

scripting

This permission is required to modify webpage styles via content scripts. It allows the extension to inject CSS and execute scripts that adjust the appearance of text on websites you visit.

activeTab

This permission is required for applying styles to the current tab. It allows the extension to access and modify the content of the webpage you are currently viewing.

host permissions (<all_urls>)

This permission is required to apply styles across websites. It allows the extension to run on any website you visit, so it can adjust text appearance according to your preferences.

Information We Collect

Nothing. Reading Style Adjuster does not collect, store, or transmit any user browsing history, personal data, or website content.

What We Do Not Collect

The extension does not collect, store, or transmit any:

  • Browsing history
  • Personal information
  • Website content
  • Usage statistics
  • Crash reports

Local Storage

The extension stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Your style preferences (font size, font family, line height, word spacing, text alignment)
  • Saved profiles
  • Site exceptions and site-specific profiles

This data is stored using the browser's chrome.storage.local API and never leaves your device.

How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect any information beyond what's stored locally on your device, there is no usage of your data beyond the functionality of the extension itself. Your settings are used solely to:

  • Apply your preferred text styles to websites
  • Remember your profiles and site-specific settings
  • Provide a consistent experience across browsing sessions

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device using the browser's built-in storage API. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any servers
  • Is protected by the browser's own security mechanisms
  • Is only accessible by the Reading Style Adjuster extension

Third-Party Services

Reading Style Adjuster does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools. The extension operates entirely within your browser and does not communicate with external servers.

User Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. Your settings remain entirely under your control on your local device.

You can:

  • View and modify your settings at any time through the extension interface
  • Export your settings to a JSON file for backup or transfer to another device
  • Reset all settings to defaults through the extension's options page
  • Uninstall the extension at any time, which will remove all stored settings

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy in the extension's repository and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Significant changes will be communicated more prominently, such as through a notification in the extension itself or through the extension's update notes.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please:

  • Open an issue in the GitHub repository
  • Contact the developer directly through GitHub
